## ğŸ’¡ Design Decisions

### Why CommonJS?
- Better npx compatibility
- Easier global installs
- No ESM module headaches
- Industry standard for CLIs

### Why These Colors?
- Green = YES favorite (positive sentiment)
- Red = NO favorite (negative sentiment)
- Cyan = Volume (neutral info)
- Yellow = Dates (time-sensitive)
- Dim = URLs (secondary info)

### Why 30s Refresh?
- Balance between real-time and API politeness
- Matches typical market movement speed
- Avoids rate limiting

### Why Top 5/10?
- Fits terminal viewport
- No pagination needed
- Focuses on most relevant
